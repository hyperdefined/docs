---
title: "SimplePlayTime/Commands"
---
These are all commands used by SimplePlayTime.

## `/playtime`
Permission: `simpleplaytime.command`

See your current playtime.

## `/playtime reload`
Permission: `simpleplaytime.reload`

Reloads the configuration.

## `/playtime reset`
Permission: `simpleplaytime.reset`

Usage: `/simpleplaytime reset <player>`

Reset a player's playtime to 0.

## `/playtime add`
Permission: `simpleplaytime.add`

Usage: `/simpleplaytime add <time>`

Add time to a player's playtime. Use the format ##d##h##m###s. Here are some examples below:

* `1h` - adds 1 hour.
* `5d4h` - adds 5 days and 4 hours.
* `30s` - add 30 seconds.
